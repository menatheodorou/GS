EMMockDeviceDelegate Protocol Reference

Conforms to NSObject
Declared in EMMockDevice.h

Overview

The mock device, through the EMMockDeviceDelegate protocol, can communicate indicators up to the application layer. By default, this delegate is set to the EMMockConnectionType this device was added to. In your subclasses of EMMockDevice, call this method on the delegate to send indicators.
